About Me.

Nitesh Rai, as a young and growing artist who is always eager to learn more from others as well as nature. He received his formal education at the Pathseekers School in Dera Beas, India from Self taught Artist Mr. Sumnish Rakwal. He then moved to Canada to pursue his studies in the fine arts at Langara College, one of Vancouver's premier institutions of higher learning. Don't be a murderer by limiting your creativity by murdering the artist inside of you, With the belief that the world needs more creative people with beautiful hearts rather than AI, without having feelings. He continually inspires others to explore their creativity and artistic skills.
